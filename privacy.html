Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 26, 2025

At FestGround, accessible from your website URL, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by FestGround and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

If you contact us directly, we may receive additional information about you such as your name, email address, phone number, the contents of the message and/or attachments you may send us.

When you use "Login with Facebook" or other social platform integrations, we collect Platform Data as authorized by you, which typically includes:

  • Public profile name and profile picture.
  • Verified email address associated with the account.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our website and services.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our platform.
  • Communicate with you for customer service or marketing updates.
  • Process VVIP concierge requests and event ticketing.
  • Find and prevent fraud.

3. Facebook Data Deletion Instructions

In compliance with Meta Platform Term 4.a and 4.b, we provide a clear way for users to request data deletion. If you wish to delete your activities for the FestGround App, you can follow these steps:

  1. Go to your Facebook Account's "Settings & Privacy". Click "Settings".
  2. Look for "Apps and Websites" and you will see all of the apps and websites you linked to your Facebook.
  3. Search and check the box next to FestGround.
  4. Click the "Remove" button.
  5. Alternatively, you may contact us at help@festground.com to request the manual deletion of any data associated with your social profile.

4. Log Files & Cookies

FestGround follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. This includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, and referring/exit pages.

5. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your Platform Data to outside parties. Your data is used strictly for the functionality of the FestGround service.

6. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at help@festground.com.
