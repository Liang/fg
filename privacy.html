Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 26, 2025

1. Universal Privacy Commitment

FestGround is committed to providing a secure and transparent environment for music fans. This policy outlines how we handle data across all our digital services, ensuring compliance with global standards and platform-specific requirements.

2. Data Collection and Usage

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. This includes:

  • Identity Data: Name and email address when you contact our VVIP concierge.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses and browser types for security and optimization.
  • Interaction Data: Information on how you interact with our festival listings.

3. Meta (Facebook) Platform Integration

In accordance with Meta Platform Terms, we process Platform Data to facilitate user authentication and personalized event services. We do not share this data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Data Deletion Instructions: As required by Meta Platform Term 4.a, users can request the removal of their data at any time:

  • Navigate to your Facebook Profile "Settings & Privacy" > "Settings" > "Apps and Websites".
  • Locate FestGround and select "Remove".
  • For manual data deletion requests, please email help@festground.com with your registered account details.

4. Information Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. Your data is stored on secure servers and is only accessible by authorized personnel.

5. Contact Our Privacy Team

If you have questions regarding this policy or our data practices, please contact us at help@festground.com.
